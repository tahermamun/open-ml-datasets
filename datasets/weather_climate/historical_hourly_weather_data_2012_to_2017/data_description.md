# Earth & Nature / Weather and Climate




## Historical Hourly Weather Data (2012-2017)

This repository contains **hourly weather data** for multiple cities in the US, Canada, and Israel from 2012 to 2017. The dataset includes temperature, humidity, pressure, wind speed, wind direction, and weather descriptions.

**Files included:**

* `city_attributes.csv` – City info (name, country, latitude, longitude)
* `temperature.csv`, `humidity.csv`, `pressure.csv`, `wind_speed.csv`, `wind_direction.csv` – Hourly measurements
* `weather_description.csv` – Weather conditions
* `data_description.md` 

**Usage:**
Ideal for **time series analysis**, **weather prediction**, or **data exploration**.
